Mission

Research for trustworthy market infrastructure

MONTIO INC is a nonprofit research organization dedicated to scientific and educational work in blockchain systems, supply chain traceability, and market infrastructure for transparent commerce.

Traceability & Provenance

Designing methods to capture product journeys from source to destination with stronger evidence, clearer ownership transfer, and faster issue resolution.

Institutional-Grade Transparency

Advancing auditable, compliance-aware data layers that align operational records with financing and risk oversight requirements.

Open Research & Education

Publishing practical frameworks and educational resources to help operators, students, and partners evaluate what works in the field.

Research Focus

Blockchain + Supply Chain Traceability

Our research examines how blockchain records, IoT signals, and QR-linked evidence can create end-to-end traceability, support immutable event history, accelerate recalls, reduce fraud vectors, and improve accountability across distributed supply networks.

Chain-of-Custody Data Model

Structuring product identity, handling events, and custody transfer metadata to preserve continuity from origin to final delivery.

IoT / QR Evidence Capture

Linking sensor and scan evidence to verifiable records for temperature, location, and handling integrity over time.

ERP Interoperability (SAP/Oracle/Microsoft)

Testing interoperability patterns so enterprise systems can exchange trusted event data without disrupting existing workflows.

Standards, Compliance & Audit Trails

Aligning schemas and controls to standards-oriented reporting and inspection needs across regulated sectors.

Institutional Finance

Trade finance and supply chain finance relevance

We investigate how auditable events and shared records can improve document integrity, proof-of-origin handling, and risk controls for institutions financing goods movement and production cycles.

Digitized Proof-of-Origin & Quality

Evaluating digital certificates and verification flows that strengthen confidence in origin and quality disclosures.

Transparent Supply Chain Events for Financing

Mapping event milestones that can support financing decisions with better visibility into goods and process status.

Compliance-Ready Reporting

Developing research-first reporting templates that reduce ambiguity and increase reviewability for oversight teams.
